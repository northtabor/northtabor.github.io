MANAGEMENT

COLE BENOIT MATTOX - FOUNDER, CEO

Cole Benoit Mattox is the founder and CEO of North Tabor Capital. Cole was born on the Upper East Side of New York City and eventually relocated to Montclair, New Jersey. Cole is currently a senior at Seton Hall Preparatory School in West Orange, New Jersey. Last summer, Cole was selected to participate in the All Star Code Summer intensive. This took place at the global investment bank Goldman Sachs. At the end of this program, Cole was named "Most Likely to Revolutionize Wall Street". This is the point at which he decided to launch North Tabor Capital. Recognizing the upcoming trends in the asset management industry, Cole decided to push the envelope and create new ways to approach portfolio management and trading.

AHNAF TAHA - CTO

Ahnaf Taha is the CTO of North Tabor Capital. A native of the Queens and Manhattan boroughs of New York City, he is a boarding senior at The Masters School in Dobbs Ferry, New York. He met Cole Mattox this past summer at the All Star Code Summer Intensive, where they were both placed in the Goldman Sachs cohort. Inspired to use his experience with computer technologies to understand the financial industry, Ahnaf joined Cole in working on North Tabor Capitol. He continues to pursue his interests in both finance and technology through his summer 2017 position as a financial analyst at Marsh & McLennan companies, and working on a variety of coding projects.
